MamaCooking

MamaCooking is a recipe website that features thousands of homemade dishes, ranging from traditional Indonesian cuisine and international meals to trendy and easy-to-make snacks. I was directly involved in the development of this website, specifically handling both the frontend and backend. On the frontend side, I built a responsive and user-friendly interface to ensure users can easily navigate and explore the content. On the backend, I managed the recipe data structure, search functionality, and implemented user account management and recipe-saving features efficiently.
